[h1]Just Another Shop Mod (JASM) | Economy & Trading[/h1]

The ultimate [b]PZ economy mod[/b] for [b]MP servers[/b] and [b]RP communities[/b]. This mod lets players and admins turn any [b]storage crate[/b] [strike]or [b]vehicle trunk[/b] (notyet)[/strike] into a fully functional, interactive [b]player shop[/b] or [b]trading post[/b].

[h2]Core Trading Features:[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Custom Player Stores:[/b] Easily register standard crates as [b]shops[/b] for your base, town, or trading outposts.
[*] [b]Dedicated Shop UI:[/b] Simple, distinct interfaces for customers to browse goods and for owners to manage stock.
[*] [b]Admin & Player Shops:[/b] Supports independent player-run stores and system-operated admin shops. (WIP)
[*] [b]Barter & Item Trade:[/b] A robust [b]item-to-item trade system[/b] perfect for hardcore survival servers. (WIP)
[*] [b]Secure & Safe Trading:[/b] Built-in lock systems prevent exploits and ensure secure [b]server economy[/b] management.
[/list]

[h1]Usage[/h1]
[quote]
1. Place an allowed crate (Wood, Metal, etc).
2. Right-click -> [b]JASM Shop[/b] to register.
3. Use [b]Manage Shop[/b] to adjust stock or [b]Open Shop UI[/b] to buy.
[/quote]

[h1]Just Another Shop[/h1]
[b]Just Another Shop Mod[/b] lets players and server admins turn ordinary storage crates into fully functional, interactive storefronts.
[list]
[*] [b]Create your own store:[/b] Easily register standard crates as shops for your base, town, or trading outposts.
[*] [b]Dedicated shop menus:[/b] Simple, distinct interfaces for customers to browse goods and for owners to manage stock.
[*] [b]Player and Admin shops:[/b] Supports independent player-run stores as well as system-operated admin shops. (WIP)
[*] [b]Secure ownership:[/b] Only the designated shop owner or server administrators can access the shop's management settings.
[*] [b]Safe trading:[/b] Built-in lock systems prevent multiple players from accessing and exploiting a shop at the exact same time.
[/list]
This mod is best suited for multiplayer servers, economy-driven survival, and roleplay communities looking for a reliable trading system.

[h3][b]Usage[/b][/h3]

- To setup a store, simply place an allowed crate (e.g., standard Wood or Metal crates).
- Right-click and navigate to the [b]JASM Shop[/b] menu to register the container.
- After registration, owners can [b]Manage Shop[/b] to adjust stock and prices, and customers can use [b]Open Shop UI[/b] to browse and buy goods.
